# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
VoteMithra is committed to providing a welcoming environment for all 
contributors regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, 
gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, 
or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards
**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the civic-tech community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment of any kind
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project 
maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
